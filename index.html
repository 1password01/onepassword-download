What is OnePassword?

OnePassword is a leading password manager designed to store and secure your login credentials, credit card details, and other sensitive information. With built-in encryption and cross-platform sync, OnePassword keeps your digital life protected on all devices including Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS.

Why Use OnePassword?

  • Secure Vault: Keeps your passwords encrypted and stored locally or in the cloud.
  • Cross-platform: Works seamlessly on all major operating systems and browsers.
  • Password Generator: Automatically creates strong, unique passwords for each login.
  • Watchtower Alerts: Alerts you to weak or compromised passwords.
  • Autofill: Quickly fills in passwords and forms to save time.
